The Problem

Google's enterprise sales team was spending hours each week crafting responses to repetitive client queries. The information existed internally — but siloed across docs, slides, and email threads. Could an AI assistant bridge that gap fast enough to actually change how a 50-person team worked?

The Approach

The system was architected as a fully autonomous Gmail agent. On every inbound email, the agent reasons over an internal knowledge base, drafts a contextual reply, sends it, and logs the interaction — all without human intervention.
