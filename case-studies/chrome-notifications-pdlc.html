Identifying the merchant channel gap

Google Merchants had strong paid acquisition but no high-converting owned channel once a user had already shown purchase intent. Email open rates were declining industry-wide. The question was whether a browser-native touchpoint could fill that gap — and whether Google had the infrastructure to make it work at merchant scale.

Scoping the problem and the user

The core problem was reframed from “build a notification system” to “make product moments feel relevant to the right user at the right time.” Target segment was narrowed to active Google Merchants already using Merchant Center — users who had purchase-intent signals already in the system. Success was defined as measurable CTR improvement over the email baseline.

Architecting the AI-native solution

The core insight: notification value comes from visual relevance, not frequency. Pairing Product Studio’s generative AI image pipeline with Chrome notification delivery let merchants push studio-quality product imagery without a creative team or photo shoot. The key design decision was to auto-select the best-performing asset variant from Product Studio and use it as the notification image.

Cross-functional execution

Led delivery across three separate engineering orgs: Merchant Center, Product Studio, and Chrome notification infrastructure — each with independent roadmaps and release cycles. Managed feature prioritization across teams, scoped the integration surface, and maintained release criteria as each dependency landed. Ran iterative reviews with a pilot merchant cohort throughout.

Shipping at merchant scale

Shipped as a core feature of Product Studio inside Google Merchant Center. The end-to-end workflow: merchant generates AI product imagery in Product Studio → image is auto-selected based on predicted performance → pushed as a Chrome notification to users who had shown purchase intent. No creative team or photo shoot required. Launched to the full Merchant Center user base.

Validating impact and scaling the playbook

Post-launch measurement confirmed the hypothesis: AI-generated product imagery in browser-native notifications significantly outperformed standard email on both CTR and engagement. The GenAI + push delivery pattern was documented as a reusable playbook and adopted across other Merchant Center notification surfaces beyond Chrome.
