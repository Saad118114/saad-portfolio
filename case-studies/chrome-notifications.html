The Problem

Google Merchants needed a high-converting owned channel beyond paid ads and email. The opportunity was Chrome Notifications — a direct browser-native touchpoint reaching users who had already shown purchase intent. But the challenge wasn’t technical. It was product: how do you make notifications feel useful rather than intrusive at 1M+ user scale?

The answer lived inside Product Studio — Google’s generative AI tool built into Merchant Center that lets merchants create professional product imagery in seconds. By pairing Product Studio’s AI-generated assets directly with Chrome Notifications, merchants could push visually rich, contextually relevant product moments at exactly the right time — without a creative team or a photo shoot.

What is Product Studio?

Product Studio is Google’s generative AI image tool inside Merchant Center. Merchants upload a product photo and describe a scene — the AI generates multiple professional backgrounds instantly. The result: studio-quality product imagery at zero cost, at scale. Read the launch post →

The Approach

The strategy was to make Chrome Notifications feel like a curated product moment rather than a generic alert. Merchants used Product Studio to generate multiple scene variants of their products — the best-performing asset was then automatically surfaced in the notification. The Outcome

Shipping Chrome Notifications with Product Studio-generated assets moved engagement metrics at a scale most PMs never touch. The combination of AI-generated imagery and browser-native delivery created a significantly higher-signal touchpoint than standard email or display retargeting.
